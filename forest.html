After you had a good look on it, try to identify what is repeating.

These are just some HTML tags that have some attributes with a value that we chose.

The tag <forest></forest> represents the main parent or container that holds the two different tags: <animals></animals> and <insects></insects>. They all behave as parents/containers and need an opening and closing tag as they hold other elements inside of them. 

  <forest>

  </forest>
